About CampusEvents

CampusEvents is a comprehensive web application designed to help students discover, create, and manage campus events seamlessly. Built with modern web technologies, it provides a user-friendly platform for the entire campus community.

Our Purpose

The primary goal of CampusEvents is to centralize event management on campus, making it easier for students to stay informed about upcoming activities and for event organizers to reach their target audience effectively. Whether it's academic seminars, cultural festivals, sports competitions, or social gatherings, CampusEvents brings everything together in one place.

Key Features

Event Discovery

Browse and search through all campus events with filtering by category, date, and venue.

Event Creation

Create and manage your own events with detailed information including title, description, date, time, venue, and category.

Event Registration

Register for events you're interested in and keep track of all your registrations in one place.

Secure Authentication

Secure user authentication with JWT tokens ensures your account and data remain protected.

Personal Dashboard

Access your personalized dashboard to view your created events and event registrations at a glance.

Event Management

Edit or delete your events with full control over the content you create.
